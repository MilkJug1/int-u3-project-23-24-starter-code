Helldivers: The Play-by-Play

You awake to your 16 birthday, something you've been waiting for all this time. You get out of your room and find your friends throwing you a party, during the party Super Earth Officals burst in and ask you a question. As is required by law by the Super Earth Government, every 16 year old is presented with the choice of joining the Helldivers and spreading Democracy. Will you join?
